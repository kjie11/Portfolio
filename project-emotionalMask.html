Emotional Mask - VR Hand-Tracking Experience (Global Game Jam 2026)

Emotional Mask is a VR hand-tracking experience exploring the psychological cost of social masking.

Players navigate different social scenarios using hand gestures that represent emotional responses. Conforming to expectations causes the virtual mask to expand, gradually obscuring vision and limiting perception. In contrast, choosing honest self-expression clears the mask, widening the field of view and brightening the environment.

The project uses embodied interaction to visualise internal emotional conflict, transforming abstract social pressure into a physical, perceptual experience.

Key Features

  • Gesture-based responses to social scenarios

Design Focus

  • Using embodiment to externalise internal conflict
  • Encouraging self-acceptance through perceptual change
