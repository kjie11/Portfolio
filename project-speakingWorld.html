Speaking World - AR & AI Companion Environment

This prototype explores loneliness through an AI-augmented AR environment where everyday objects become conversational companions. Instead of a single virtual assistant, the surrounding space itself responds to the user — fridges, sofas, and even food are given voice and personality. The project reimagines the environment as a form of ambient companionship, questioning how spatial AI might reshape our experience of presence and connection. An integrated AR graffiti feature allows users to leave expressive marks, reinforcing personal presence within the space.

Key Features

  • AI-powered conversational objects
  • AR interaction with everyday items
  • AR graffiti for personal expression

Design Focus

  • Environment as companion - turning environments into responsive social spaces
