Hi, I'm Ruyan Qin.

I am an XR Designer and UX Researcher based in London, specializing in immersive interaction and embodied experiences in VR/AR environments.

My work bridges technical prototyping and user-centered research. I design and implement tangible interaction systems in Unity, conduct user studies, and iteratively refine experiences to transform experimental ideas into polished immersive products.

My projects include tangible AR graffiti research (paper accepted at DIS 2026), physics-based VR experiences, and experimental WebXR systems.
