AR Escape Room

An AR escape room experience where players solve spatial puzzles embedded within their physical surroundings. Virtual objects are anchored to real-world space, requiring players to move, observe, and interact with both digital and physical elements.

Key Features

  • Spatially anchored virtual objects interacting with real-world surfaces
  • Passthrough-based puzzles combining physical movement and digital manipulation
  • Integrated machine learning–based handwritten digit recognition
  • QR code detection used as interactive puzzle triggers

Design Focus

  • Integrating AR affordances: spatial alignment, real-world passthrough, and object recognition
  • Creating seamless interactions between virtual and physical puzzle elements
  • Enhancing player immersion through spatial puzzle-solving mechanics

My Role

  • Team Lead: Facilitated project coordination
  • Level Designer: Conceptualized and mapped spatial AR puzzle layouts
  • Gameplay Programmer: Implemented ML-based digit recognition and QR triggers
